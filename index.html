About Me

Building the Future with Precision & Innovation

I'm Ahmed Hesham, Founder of "The Great Home" with over than 6 years of experience in finishing engineering, project management, and construction supervision. My passion lies in creating sustainable and innovative solutions that stand the test of time.

Throughout my career, I've worked on diverse projects ranging from high level residential buildings to small reparation. I believe in combining technical expertise with creative problem-solving to deliver exceptional results that exceed client expectations.

The ultimate goal is 100% customer satisfaction. This requires us to do everything necessary to serve the customer by developing innovative and sustainable ideas and solutions at the lowest possible cost.
